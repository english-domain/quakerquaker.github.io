A Dedicated Domain for Quaker Collaboration

QuakerQuaker.com

Are you a Quaker who supports the Quaker movement and wishes to contribute to online collaboration within the Quaker community? QuakerQuaker.com is a domain name that can serve as a valuable platform for your vision.

We are "not interested in selling" this domain name for profit. Instead, we are willing to provide it to dedicated and capable Quakers who have innovative ideas for using it to support the Quaker community. If you are a Quaker with a vision for this domain, we want to hear from you.

Please provide your ideas and plans if you are interested.
